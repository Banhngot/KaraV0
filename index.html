About KARA

Who will speak for the children? KARA does. We're America’s leading voice for at-risk youth, turning advocacy into real-time action.

Our Story

Our Mission

KARA was founded to support people, programs, and policies that improve the lives of abused and neglected children across the United States. We advocate for at-risk youth through real-time data, training, and technology — empowering communities to protect children before it's too late.

"Who Will Speak For The Children?"

Our History

Since our founding in 2001, KARA has operated with a dedicated board, advisors, and hundreds of student interns and community volunteers. We've spoken nationally on child protection issues, building a legacy of impact. Past leaders include pioneering voices like David Strand, Joe McCarthy, and Thomas Carter MD, who laid the foundation for our work today.
